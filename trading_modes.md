# Trading Platform Features

## 1. Classic Mode
- Standard trading interface with candlestick charts.
- Timeframes range from 5 seconds to 1 day.
- Traders can place Market Orders or Buy Price Orders.
- Includes risk controls like Take Profit and Stop Loss.

## 2. Signals Mode
- Integrates Pocket Option signals directly into the trading chart.
- Signals are generated from price action, support/resistance, and market structure.
- Traders can copy signals with one click or confirm entries manually.

## 3. OTC (Over-the-Counter) Mode
- Allows trading outside regular market hours.
- OTC assets are synthetic instruments created by Pocket Option.
- Useful for practicing strategies when global markets are closed.

## 4. AI Mode
- Automated analysis overlay that suggests potential trade entries.
- Designed for beginners who want guided decision-making.
- Can be toggled on/off from the trading panel.

## 5. Customizable Chart Views
- Multiple chart types: Heiken Ashi, Line, Bar, Candlestick.
- Technical overlays: Fibonacci lines, Bollinger Bands, Moving Averages.
- Adjustable timeframes and zoom for micro or macro analysis.

## 6. MetaTrader Integration
- Option to switch between Pocket Option native charts and MetaTrader 4/5.
- Provides advanced indicators and external strategy testing.
